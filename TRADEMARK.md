# Trademark Policy



"OpenGovCon", "OGRE Team", and associated logos are trademarks of their respective owners. Use of the trademarks is not granted by the open-source licenses of OpenGovCon codebases.

Forks and derivative projects must not use OpenGovCon trademarks in a way that implies:

- endorsement

- affiliation

- certification



Permitted usage:

- factual statements such as "forked from OpenGovCon"

- compatibility claims, if accurate



Prohibited usage:

- marketing a fork as an official OpenGovCon release

- using OpenGovCon branding in a way that confuses users



This policy exists to prevent brand capture by proprietary vendors.
